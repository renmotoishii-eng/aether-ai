# AETHER AI — Executive Summary

## The One-Liner
**AETHER AI** is an AI automation studio for mid-market e-commerce brands — replacing product photography studios, customer service teams, and content agencies with one integrated subscription platform.

---

## The Problem
Mid-market e-commerce brands ($2M–$50M/yr) can't afford enterprise AI but compete against brands spending millions on photography, support, and content. Photography costs $50–$500/SKU. Support consumes 30–40% of headcount. Content requires specialized writers per channel.

## The Solution

| Product | What It Does | Client Savings |
|---|---|---|
| **Aether Visuals** | AI product photography via ComfyUI/Flux | $0.35/image vs $50–$500 studio |
| **Aether Agents** | 24/7 AI customer service agents | 80% reduction in ticket volume |
| **Aether Content** | SEO descriptions, blogs, social posts | 10x faster, 90% cheaper |

- 340,000 businesses in our core ICP
