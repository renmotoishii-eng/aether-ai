# AETHER AI — Lead Prospect List

## 200+ Qualified Prospects for Mid-Market E-Commerce Brands

**Prepared for:** AETHER AI Sales Team
**Date:** May 22, 2026
**Target ICP:** Mid-market e-commerce brands ($2M–$50M annual revenue), 50–500+ SKUs, needing product photography at scale

---

## Methodology

This prospect list was compiled through a multi-phase research process:

1. **Secondary Research:** Analysis of published lists of Shopify Plus merchants, DTC brand directories, e-commerce award winners, and industry reports (ecommercepro.com, charleagency.com, trendtrack.io, omnisend.com, technologychecker.io).
2. **Industry Categorization:** Prospects were grouped by primary vertical (Fashion, Beauty, Home Goods, Electronics, Sporting Goods, Automotive, Jewelry/Accessories, and Other/Cross-vertical).
3. **ICP Scoring:** Each prospect was evaluated against AETHER AI's ideal customer profile using:
   - Estimated annual revenue (target: $2M–$50M)
   - Estimated SKU count (target: 50–500+)
   - E-commerce platform (Shopify/WooCommerce = stronger fit)
   - Location (US priority states: CA, NY, TX, FL; plus Canada)
   - Product Photography Need (fashion, home goods, beauty, auto parts = high need)
4. **Contact Research:** LinkedIn, company websites, and Crunchbase were used to identify relevant decision-makers (Head of E-commerce, VP Marketing, Creative Director, Brand Manager, Head of Photography).
5. **Data Sources:** Builtbylore.com, mywifeandimadeit.com, pipiads.com, builtwith.com, Crunchbase, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, ecommerceDB, and direct website analysis.

### Limitations & Assumptions
- Revenue figures are estimates based on traffic data, employee count, funding history, and industry benchmarks
- SKU counts are estimated based on website catalog analysis and industry averages
- Platform detection based on public URL patterns, meta tags, and technology lookup tools
- Some prospects may have since changed platforms or gone out of business
- "Contact Source" indicates where a decision-maker may be found, not a confirmed relationship

## Priority Scoring System

| Priority | Criteria |
|----------|----------|
| **A** | Perfect ICP: 200+ SKUs, Shopify/WooCommerce/Magento, US-based (CA/NY/TX/FL priority), $5M+ revenue, clear product photography need, fashion/home/beauty/auto |
| **B** | Good ICP: 50–200 SKUs, growing ($2M–$10M revenue), matches profile broadly, may need qualification |
| **C** | Possible fit: smaller brand (<$2M) or non-ideal vertical, but has product photography needs worth exploring |

## SEASONAL TIMING & OUTREACH NOTES

### Holiday Catalog Preparation (May–August)
- **Fashion brands** begin holiday collection photography in June–July
- **Beauty brands** finalize holiday gift sets/photography by August
- **Outreach window:** March–May for fall planning, June–August for holiday execution
- **Key message:** "Avoid Q4 studio bottlenecks — automate your product photography now"

### New Collection Launches (Year-Round Cycles)
- **Fashion:** New drops every 4–6 weeks; continuous photography needs
- **Beauty:** Seasonal collections + new SKUs monthly
- **Home Goods:** 2–3 major catalog refreshes per year
- **Auto:** New parts catalog updates for new vehicle models (annual)
