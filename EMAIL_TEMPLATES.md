## TEMPLATE 1: COLD INTRO

**Subject line variants:**
- (A) "Cut your [$COST/mo] product photography by 80%"
- (B) "[$BRAND_NAME], your product photos are costing you sales"
- (C) "47 seconds vs 5 days -- AI product photography is here"

"Most brands I talk to are spending more on product photography than on ad creative -- and getting worse results from both. Here is why."

## TEMPLATE 2: FOLLOW-UP

**Send trigger:** 3 days after cold intro (if no reply)

**Subject line variants:**
- (A) "Quick thought on [$BRAND_NAME] product photos"
- (B) "I noticed something about your [$PRODUCT_CATEGORY] shots"
- (C) "Re: Your product photography"

**Open rate note:** Subject A is 36-42 chars -- under the 50-char mobile cutoff. Referencing their specific product shows you looked, not just blasted.

---

**Body:**

I took a look at your [$PRODUCT_CATEGORY] product pages. Your hero images are doing a lot of the heavy lifting, but here is what I noticed:

-- Your bounce rate is likely higher on products with only 1-2 angles

I am not asking for a call (yet). Just wanted to share what I saw. If you want, reply with a link to your top 3 products and I will send back AETHER-generated alternatives free of charge. No strings.

Best,
[SENDER_NAME]

**Alt opening line:**
"Hi [FIRST_NAME] -- following up on my note from Tuesday. I know you are busy, so I will keep this tight."

**P.S. line:**
"P.S. One of our customers replaced ALL of their product photos in a weekend. 200 SKUs, $280 total. Their designer is still not sure what to do with her freed-up time."

## Quick Reference: Template Usage Sequence

| Stage | Template | Timing | Goal |
|-------|----------|--------|------|
| 1 | Cold Intro (T1) | Day 0 | Hook with cost-saving math + free sample offer |
| 2 | Follow-Up (T2) | Day 3 | Specific observation about their site + soften CTA |
| 3 | Design Partner (T3) | Day 7 | Exclusive offer for high-value prospects |
| 4 | Re-Engagement (T4) | Day 37 | New feature hook + deadline urgency |
| 5 | Warm Intro (T5) | Any time | Leverage referral for instant credibility |

Note: If a prospect responds at any stage, stop the sequence and move to a conversational reply. Do NOT send the next template in the sequence.

## Best Practices for AETHER AI Email Outreach

1. **Always include the free sample offer.** Replying with a product URL and getting back an AI-generated hero shot is low-friction and proves the product immediately.
2. **Use [MUTUAL_CONTACT] references whenever possible.** Warm intros convert at 3-5x the rate of cold emails.
3. **Keep subject lines under 50 characters.** 72% of emails are first opened on mobile, and mobile inboxes truncate at ~50-60 chars.
4. **One CTA per email.** Multiple CTAs dilute response rates. The free sample offer is the strongest CTA for cold email.
5. **Personalize the body, not just the subject.** Reference their specific product category, estimated SKU count, or observable issues on their site.
6. **Track open and reply rates.** Benchmarks for B2B SaaS cold email: 20-35% open rate, 2-5% reply rate. If you are below 15% open rate, test new subject lines. If you are above 5% reply rate, you are doing well.
7. **Design Partner program is your best conversion lever.** 50% off + direct access to engineering + case study = a hard-to-refuse value stack for the right prospects.
